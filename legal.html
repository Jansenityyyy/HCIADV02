    Privacy Policy

    Overview

    Amore Academy values the trust you place in us when you share your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the data of students, parents, guardians, and visitors in compliance with applicable Philippine law.

    This policy applies to the Amore Academy website and all related digital services we operate. By using our website, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

    This policy is governed by the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) and its implementing rules and regulations. We are registered with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) of the Philippines.

    Collection

    Information We Collect

    Information You Provide

    Submitted through forms, applications, emails, and registration — including names, contact details, and academic records.

    Automatically Collected

    Technical data such as IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, and session duration — used only for site improvement.

    Personal Information We May Request

    When you engage with our admissions process, contact us, or register for school programs, we may ask for:

    • Full name, date of birth, and gender
    • Home address and contact number
    • Email address of parent or guardian
    • Academic records or prior school information
    • Government-issued identification (for enrollment purposes only)

    Sensitive Personal Information

    In limited cases related to student health, safety, or learning support, we may collect sensitive information such as medical conditions or special learning needs. This is handled with the highest level of care and is only disclosed on a strict need-to-know basis.

    Use of Data

    How We Use Your Information

    We process personal data only for legitimate and specified purposes. The information we collect is used to:

    • Process and evaluate admissions applications
    • Facilitate enrollment and manage student records
    • Communicate school announcements, events, and important updates
    • Improve and maintain our website and digital services
    • Ensure the safety and well-being of all students and staff
    • Comply with legal obligations under Philippine law
    • Respond to inquiries, feedback, or complaints

    We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to advertisers or marketing companies. Your data is used only to serve you and operate our school.

    Disclosure

    Information Sharing & Disclosure

    We treat your information with discretion. We only share data in the following limited, necessary circumstances:

    1

    Service Providers — Trusted vendors who support our operations (e.g., learning management systems, email platforms) under strict confidentiality agreements.

    2

    Government Authorities — The Department of Education (DepEd) and other regulatory agencies as required by the K-12 law and Philippine educational regulations.

    3

    Legal Compliance — When required by court order, subpoena, or applicable law to protect the rights and safety of our community.

    4

    Emergency Situations — To protect the vital interests of a student or any person in situations involving immediate health or safety risks.

    Your Rights

    Your Data Privacy Rights

    Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173), every data subject has protected rights. As a member of the Amore Academy community, you are entitled to:

    ✓

    Right to Be Informed — Know what personal data is being collected, why, and how it is being processed.

    ✓

    Right to Access — Request a copy of your personal data that we hold, along with details of how it has been used.

    ✓

    Right to Correct — Dispute and request correction of any inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated personal information.

    ✓

    Right to Erasure — Request the deletion or blocking of your data when it is no longer necessary for the purpose for which it was collected.

    ✓

    Right to Object — Object to the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances, including for direct marketing.

    ✓

    Right to Data Portability — Receive a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used format where technically feasible.

    To exercise any of these rights, please contact our designated Data Privacy Officer. We aim to respond to all verified requests within fifteen (15) business days.

    Cookies

    Cookies & Tracking

    Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to enhance your experience, remember preferences, and analyze how visitors interact with our site. We use only essential and analytics cookies.

    Essential Cookies

    Necessary for the website to function. These enable navigation, form submissions, and basic security features.

    Analytics Cookies

    Help us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve content and navigation over time.

    You may disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

    Security

    Data Retention & Security

    We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by law. Student academic records are retained in accordance with DepEd regulations. Inquiry and application data from non-enrolled individuals is deleted after two (2) years unless otherwise required.

    How We Protect Your Data

    We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard personal data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. These include:

    • Secure, encrypted transmission (HTTPS) across our entire website
    • Access controls limiting data to authorized personnel only
    • Regular security reviews and staff data privacy training
    • Secure physical storage for printed records and documents

    While we employ reasonable security measures, no digital system is completely invulnerable. We encourage you to protect your account credentials and contact us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access to your information.

    Accessibility

    Our Commitment

    Amore Academy believes that education belongs to everyone — and that commitment extends to our digital presence. We are dedicated to ensuring that our website is accessible to all people, including those with disabilities.

    We are actively working toward conformance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA — the internationally recognized standard for accessible web content. This is an ongoing effort, and we continuously review and improve our website with accessibility in mind.

    Our accessibility efforts are also aligned with Republic Act No. 7277 (the Magna Carta for Persons with Disability), which upholds the rights and privileges of persons with disabilities in the Philippines.

    Features

    Accessibility Features

    We have implemented the following features to support users with a range of needs and preferences:

    Screen Reader Support

    Semantic HTML, ARIA landmarks, descriptive alt text, and form labels ensure compatibility with screen reader software.

    Keyboard Navigation

    All interactive elements are fully navigable using only a keyboard, with clear and visible focus indicators throughout.

    Color Contrast

    Text and interface elements maintain at least a 4.5:1 contrast ratio against their background for comfortable reading.

    Scalable Text

    Text on all pages can be resized up to 200% using standard browser zoom without loss of content or functionality.

    Mobile Responsive

    A fully responsive layout ensures the website is usable and legible across phones, tablets, and desktop screens of all sizes.

    Plain Language

    Content is written in clear, straightforward language appropriate for students, parents, and the general community.

    Standards

    Conformance Standards

    We measure and guide our accessibility work against the following recognized standards and legal frameworks:

    WCAG 2.1 AA
    Web Content Accessibility Guidelines International standard by the W3C covering perceivability, operability, understandability, and robustness of web content.
    In Progress
    RA 7277
    Magna Carta for Persons with Disability Philippine legislation protecting the rights of persons with disabilities to equal access to education and services.
    Active
    Section 508
    U.S. Rehabilitation Act Standards Referenced as a supplementary benchmark for electronic and information technology accessibility.
    In Progress

    We acknowledge that some areas of our website may not yet fully meet all standards. We are committed to addressing known gaps and appreciate your patience as we continue to improve.

    Alternatives

    Alternative Formats & Assistance

    If you require information from our website in an alternative format — such as large print, audio recording, simplified language, or Braille — please contact our office and we will do our best to accommodate your request in a timely and respectful manner.

    We also offer in-person assistance at our Administration Office for community members who have difficulty navigating our website or completing online forms. No one should feel excluded from the Amore Academy experience due to accessibility barriers.

    Amore Academy does not discriminate on the basis of disability. We are committed to providing equal access to all of our programs, services, and communications — online and in person.
