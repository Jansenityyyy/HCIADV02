How to Enroll

Procedure for Enrollment

Our enrollment process is designed to be clear and accessible. Follow these steps to complete your application and secure your place at Amore Academy.

1

Download or Pick Up the Application Form

Obtain the official Application for Admission form from the Registrar's Office or download it from the school website. Fill it out completely and legibly.

2

Prepare All Required Documents

Gather all documentary requirements specific to your program level (JHS or SHS). Ensure all copies are certified true copies where required. See the Requirements section below for the complete list.

3

Submit Requirements to the Registrar's Office

Bring your completed application form and all supporting documents to the Registrar's Office during office hours (Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM–5:00 PM). Incomplete submissions will not be processed.

4

Take the Entrance Examination

All applicants are required to take the Amore Academy Entrance Examination. You will be informed of the schedule upon submission of your requirements. Bring a valid ID and two pencils on the day of the exam.

5

Wait for Admission Results

Results will be released within five to seven working days after the examination. Successful applicants will receive an official Notice of Acceptance via their registered email address.

6

Pay the Enrollment Fee and Confirm Your Slot

Upon receiving your Notice of Acceptance, proceed to the Cashier's Office to pay the enrollment fee and officially secure your slot. Slots are reserved on a first-come, first-served basis after acceptance.

7

Attend the New Student Orientation

Newly enrolled students are required to attend the New Student Orientation before the first day of classes. Details on the orientation schedule will be included in your enrollment confirmation.

What You Need to Prepare

Admission Requirements

Grades 7 – 10
Junior High School

Junior High School Requirements

The following documents are required for all Grade 7 applicants to Amore Academy. Please ensure all copies are complete and accurate before submitting to the Registrar's Office.

Academic Records
  • Form 138 (Report Card) — Original copy from Grade 6 with complete final grades
  • Form 137 (Permanent Record) — For transferees from other schools
  • Certificate of Completion — Issued by the Elementary School principal
Personal Documents
  • PSA-authenticated Birth Certificate — Original copy
  • 2x2 ID Photos — Four (4) recent, identical, white background
  • Barangay Clearance — Issued within the last three months
School Requirements
  • Completed Application for Admission Form
  • Photocopy of Parent or Guardian's valid government-issued ID
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character — Signed by previous school principal
Note: All documents must be submitted in a long brown envelope, clearly labeled with the applicant's complete name and program level. Incomplete submissions will not be accepted. For questions, please contact the Registrar's Office at (02) 8123-4567 or email registrar@amoreacademy.edu.ph.
Grades 11 – 12
Senior High School

Senior High School Requirements

The following documents are required for all Grade 11 applicants to Amore Academy. Applicants must also indicate their preferred SHS Track and Strand on the Application Form.

Academic Records
  • Form 138 (Report Card) — Original copy from Grade 10 with complete final grades
  • Form 137 (Permanent Record) — For transferees from other JHS schools
  • Certificate of Junior High School Completion — Issued by school principal
Personal Documents
  • PSA-authenticated Birth Certificate — Original copy
  • 2x2 ID Photos — Four (4) recent, identical, white background
  • Barangay Clearance — Issued within the last three months
School Requirements
  • Completed Application for Admission Form (indicate preferred Track and Strand)
  • Photocopy of Parent or Guardian's valid government-issued ID
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character — Signed by JHS principal or adviser
  • Personal Essay — One page, topic: "Why I chose this SHS Track and how it will help me achieve my goals"
Note: All documents must be submitted in a long brown envelope, clearly labeled with the applicant's complete name and preferred SHS Track and Strand. Applicants with special academic distinctions (honors, awards) are encouraged to attach supporting documents. For questions, contact the Registrar's Office at (02) 8123-4567 or email registrar@amoreacademy.edu.ph.
